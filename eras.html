About the Game

Eras Zombie Invasion is a Zombie Macro RTS (Real-Time Strategy) custom game, originally made by Anteep. It is currently split between two major game communities in Warcraft III and Starcraft II. In Eras Zombie Invasion, you get to play as a Medieval Country to defend against a rapidly advancing zombie horde coming from the heart of Asia. As time passes, you build up your country and pass through ages in history all the way to the future. Or play as one of the Zombie Masters, evolve your zombies, and enslave the human race.

Gameplay

Players will explore various historical periods, collecting weapons and resources to survive the apocalypse.
